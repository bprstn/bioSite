Exclusive Interview with Chloe

1. What’s your favorite thing to do on weekends?

I love exploring new restaurants and going to brunch with friends!

2. If you could travel anywhere in the world, where would you go?

Definitely Greece! I love the beaches, food, and history.

3. What’s your go-to coffee order?

Caramel Cappuccino with oat milk. YUM!

4. What’s one fashion item you can’t live without?

My favorite designer feather sandals.

5. What’s your favorite type of food?

Mediterranean food. I just love all the flavors.

6. What’s your biggest pet peeve?

Slow walkers or when Brian stops walking to text.

7. What’s a hobby you’ve always wanted to try?

Trapeze! It just looks so fun!

8. If you could have dinner with any celebrity, who would it be?

Britney Spears. Her music raised me!

9. What’s your dream job?

Owning my own boutique! I love fashion and styling.

10. What’s one piece of advice you live by?

“My mom said smart people learn from other's mistakes, ordinary people learn on their own, and stupid people never learn.” Haha, it's something I've always kept in mind growing up.
