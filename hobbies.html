Chloe’s Passion for Food & Fashion

Chloe has a love for both **exploring new restaurants** and **cooking gourmet meals** at home. Being her best friend means I'm always tagging along on culinary adventures to places I’d never have discovered on my own. Many of these restaurants are so exclusive that it takes **months** just to secure a reservation! Thanks to Chloe, I’ve fallen in love with incredible dining experiences.

But Chloe isn’t just a foodie—she’s also an **amazing cook**. She loves trying out new recipes and preparing extravagant meals for her friends and her husband, Andrew. One thing she’s absolutely known for? **Edible glitter.** If she can add sparkle to a dish, she will!

Beyond food, Chloe has an impeccable sense of **style**—**classy, elegant, and always on point.** Whether she's dressed up for a night out or styling casual looks, she does everything with grace and confidence.
